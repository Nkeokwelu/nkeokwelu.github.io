GB Power Mix Evolution Dashboard

This interactive Tableau dashboard explores the GB's ongoing energy transition: the steady decline in fossil fuel reliance (from dominant in 2010 to 32% in 2023), the rise of renewables (led by wind at 28.9% in 2023), stable nuclear contributions (14.5%), growing imports (12.4%), and a dramatic drop in average carbon intensity — from peaks over 500 gCO₂/kWh down to 149 gCO₂/kWh in 2023.
